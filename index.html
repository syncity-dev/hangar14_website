Gabrijela Bađun

Functional Fitness / CrossFit

Hi, I’m Gabrijela — I hold a Master’s degree in Kinesiology in Education and Fitness, and I’m a certified Functional Fitness Instructor.

I’m deeply passionate about CrossFit and the Hybrid style of training. I’ve been actively training and competing for over 8 years, which has allowed me to develop a strong understanding and knowledge base in strength, power, and endurance training.

This experience has also helped me refine my ability to plan and program workouts effectively, recognize technical details, and understand the importance of an individual approach and adapting to different fitness levels.

My goal at Hangar 14 is to share my passion and motivation with others — to help people fall in love with the process of physical training and feel healthier and happier.
